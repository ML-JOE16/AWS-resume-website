Intro

Hello and welcome to my website!

I am graduate from Wentworth Institute of Technology, where I majored in Mechanical Engineering and minored in Business Management. During my time at Wentworth, I gained hands-on experience in the mechanical engineering field through various labs and team-based projects. These experiences allowed me to develop strong collaboration and communication skills, which were crucial in delivering high-quality work. Additionally, I have worked with several tools, including SolidWorks, MATLAB, and Microsoft Office, to successfully complete these projects.

Following graduation, I joined Raytheon as a Systems Engineer I, where I worked on Logistics Management Information (LMI) databases for international defense clients—developing a keen eye for detail and data integrity. I later led a team supporting the Patriot Foreign Military Sales (FMS) program, gaining valuable experience in project leadership, budgeting, and personnel management. In 2021, I was promoted to Systems Engineer II and took on the responsibility of leading material availability efforts for the Zumwalt program. Despite being new to the domain, I quickly adapted and drove efficiencies through a strong work ethic and commitment to continuous learning

In 2022, I transitioned to Odyssey Systems, where I currently support the United States Air Force in qualifying commercial security systems for base-wide deployment across the country. I also hold a U.S. government secret security clearance for this role. This position sparked my growing interest in cybersecurity, motivating me to further expand my knowledge through structured learning

Furthermore, I’ve completed the MIT Professional Education Program in Applied Data Science, where I gained hands-on experience building machine learning models in Python. I’ve also earned various certifications including AWS Cloud Practitioner, AWS Solutions Architect, and AWS Machine Learning Specialty. More recently, I have just received my CompTIA Security+ certification to strengthen my understanding of cybersecurity principles, risk management, and network security best practices.

I am confident that my background in engineering, project management, and cybersecurity would make me useful asset. I am also eager to further expand my knowledge and continue growing professionally.
